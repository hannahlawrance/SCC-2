We’re a socially-conscious surf collective connecting travellers with local surf coaches in Bali. We offer private lessons you can book on the fly. Hit us up and connect.

Did you know the average surf coach in Bali earns $8 AUD for a two-hour surf lesson?

That's on average, $40 a week, $160 a month, $1,920 a year.

While the living conditions in Bali are cheap, they're not that cheap.

An average meal from a Warung costs about $2. That's $6 left for two more meals, water, electricity and petrol.

Surf coaches in Bali struggle to make ends meet. They’re forced to work multiple jobs, long hours, or give up surf coaching all together. Surf coaching is just not sustainable.

We love surfing in Bali but felt bad enjoying a cheap surf lesson knowing our surf coach might not be able to afford their next meal. Kind of ruins the experience, don’t you agree?

Skilled surf coaches should be able to make a decent living.

So we decided to create a surf collective that pays surf coaches a fair wage, while making it easy and affordable for travellers to learn how to surf.

When you book a surfing lesson with Surf Connect Co. you’re making your surf coach’s life a little easier. Our sessions are affordable for Aussies, while still being fair to the surf pros.

Let's connect and surf ethically.

Founded by Australian girl Hannah Lawrance in November 2018.
