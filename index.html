What is Surf Connect Co.?

Oh, hey! We’re Surf Connect Co. A socially-conscious surf collective connecting travellers with local surf coaches in Bali. We create casual surf experiences for your Bali adventures.

You pick the day and the beach. We’ll connect you with an available surf coach. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned pro, your local surfer will show you the waves.

Why us?

We’re local and legit

Our surf coaches know the water and the conditions. We vet them so you know they’re legit.

We’re ethical

We believe surf coaches in Bali should get paid properly. You’ll know where your money is going.

We’re casual and convenient

You surf when you want. Where you want. We fit around your travel schedule.

How it works

Step 1 – Head to the booking page

Step 2 – Tell us your level and where you're based

Step 4 - Pick how long you want to surf and what day

Step 5 - We'll connect you to a freelance instructor
